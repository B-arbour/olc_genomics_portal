Explore Data

Identify SeqIDs for analysis. Search for your genus of interest at differing levels of assembly quality and receive list of SeqIDs.
More search options coming soon - email andrew.low@canada.ca with any requests.


Explore Data

Get Data

Retrive copies of files stored at OLC to analyze locally. Enter desired SeqIDs for either assemblies (FASTA) or raw data (FASTQ), a link to those files will be created as well as a shareable distribution link. Files created are available for seven (7) days.

Get Data

Assembly

For submission of raw sequence data for assembly. You can also use this to get reports and assemblies from previous runs.




Assembly

Find Genes

Implements OLC's GeneSeekr tool to search for any gene of interest in our collection. Specify which sequences you want to search using SeqIDs, and also provide a gene (or genes) you want to search for in FASTA format.
